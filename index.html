About

MonsterGymnasium "where you can get fit" established in the year 1940. We had alot of famous bodybuilders over the years like Arnold Schwarznegger, Phill Heath, CT Ali Fletcher, Ronnie Coleman, Jay Cutler and others. MonsterGymnasium has been the place for training beasts and monsters over the years where they helped build this place and keep it running.

MonsterGymnasium started as a small fitness center at first in the year 1940 Where it could barely fit 50 people at the same time. In the year 1947, we got a loan from the bank and with the help of friends and family we got to expand the gym to fit a lot more people, more machines and equipments, made more marketing campaigns and commercials to reach as much people as possible.

In the year 1955 famous bodybuilders started to come and train at our gym which is a great honour to us. We then took the opportunity to expand even more to be one of the biggest and known gyms in the world so we can train and help as many people as we can. Our gym was never about making money; it was about helping and training as many people as we can so they can become fit and even, one day, become bodybuilders themselves; and that's exactly what we did.

MonsterGymnasium helped in making a lot of bodybuilders who won many championships like Mr. Olympia, Mr. World, etc...

MonsterGymnasium welcomes women as well to become fit and join our family to train and, if they want, become bodybuilders themselves and win championships.

MonsterGymnasium also offers job opportunities to become coach, physical therapist, massage therapist and much more. All you have to do is fill a form at the reception and bring a copy of your speciality report. So we can evaluate you and put you in the right position we need you the most.

MonsterGymnasium welcomes you to become part of the family, To join us as a trainer or trainee. So what are you waiting for. Come join us now, we are waiting for you.
